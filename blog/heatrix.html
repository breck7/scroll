Not just a library, it's a microlanguage!

heatrix is a microlang that let's you customize your visualizations using "directives":

Adjust the height/width of individual cells, columns, or rows:

Scroll
PLDB
400
700
heatrix h20;Scroll h20;PLDB 400 700

Add links:

Scroll
PLDB
400
700
heatrix h20;Scroll;https://scroll.pub h20;PLDB;https://pldb.io 400 700

Add labels:

Canada
USA
Mexico
heatrix 38'Canada 333'USA 127'Mexico

Use numbers as labels:

2021
